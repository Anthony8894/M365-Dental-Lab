# overview

Amelie Griffith is leaving for a vacation and will not have access to her email. She had let the team know that she is expecting important emails that needs attention.


## solution

Since Amelia will be out of office, we can either turn her mailbox into a shared mailbox, enable forwarding, or manage read permissions. 

## shared mailbox

1. open **exchange admin center**
2. under recipients > click mailboxes
   1. all mailboxes will appear on this page including user mailboxes.
   2. you can identify type of mailbox by looking under recipient type
![1]
3. click on the user
4. click on **others** and go ahead and click on **convert to shared mailbox**
![2]
